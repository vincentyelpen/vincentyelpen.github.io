--- title: "Research Topics in Computer Science and Mathematics" excerpt: "A collection of research topics exploring various aspects of Computer Science and Mathematic
" collection: portfolio ---
  1. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

    Explore advanced algorithms in AI and ML, focusing on deep learning, reinforcement learning, and their applications in various fields such as healthcare, finance, and autonomous systems.

  2. Quantum Computing and Cryptography

    Study the principles of quantum computing and its implications for cryptography, including quantum key distribution and post-quantum cryptographic algorithms.

  3. Data Science and Big Data Analytics

    Investigate the methods and tools for analyzing large datasets, focusing on predictive analytics, data mining, and machine learning techniques in big data environments.

  4. Computational Complexity and Algorithms

    Research the theoretical foundations of computational complexity, including NP-completeness, approximation algorithms, and the development of efficient algorithms for complex problems.

  5. Cybersecurity and Information Assurance

    Examine strategies for protecting information systems from cyber threats, including network security, encryption methods, and risk management in cybersecurity.

  6. Mathematical Modeling and Simulation

    Explore the use of mathematical models to simulate real-world systems, with applications in fields such as physics, engineering, biology, and economics.

  7. Computational Neuroscience

    Investigate the use of computational models to understand brain function, including neural networks, brain-computer interfaces, and the simulation of cognitive processes.

  8. Cloud Computing and Distributed Systems

    Study the design and implementation of distributed systems, focusing on cloud computing architectures, scalability, and fault tolerance in large-scale systems.

  9. Graph Theory and Network Analysis

    Research the properties of graphs and their applications in network analysis, including social networks, biological networks, and communication networks.

  10. Numerical Methods and Scientific Computing

    Explore the development and application of numerical algorithms for solving mathematical problems, with a focus on scientific computing and high-performance computing environments.
